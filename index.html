Lemon Bars - $28/dozen

Lemon bars are classic, handheld desserts featuring a vibrant, tangy-sweet lemon curd filling baked on a buttery, crisp shortbread crust. Sweet and citrusy, amazing for people with a sour sweet tooth! They are refreshing, gooey, and indulgent treats perfect for spring.

Nutella Blondies - $25/dozen

Nutella blondies are indulgent, rich, and fudgy dessert bars, characterized by a dense, brown-sugar butterscotch base swirled with creamy chocolate-hazelnut spread. They feature a shiny, crinkled top, soft gooey center, and intense hazelnut-chocolate flavors. Perfect with Ice cream!

Key Lime Pie - $32/whole pie

Key lime pie is a classic American dessert originating from Key West, Florida, typically featuring a tart custard made from Key lime juice, sweetened condensed milk, and egg yolks. It is known for its tart, creamy filling and pale yellow color. It is a signature dessert of Key West, Florida. A spring must-have!

Mocha Tiramisu - $8/slice

An Italian dessert that elevates the classic by layering espresso-soaked ladyfingers with a rich cocoa-mascarpone mixture. A velvety treat balancing bitter espresso, sweet cream, and deep chocolate, with cocoa powder and chocolate shavings on top!
